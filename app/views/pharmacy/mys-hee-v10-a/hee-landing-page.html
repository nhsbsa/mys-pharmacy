Pre-Registration Trainee Pharmacy Technicians (PTPTs) and Pharmacist Foundation Training Programme (Yr.5) Allocation of Funding

HEE is undertaking management of the Pre-registration Trainee Pharmacy Technician training programme (PTPT) in England.

This is part of a wider national Pharmacy Education Reform Programme, which includes the quality management of and investment in Pharmacist and Pharmacy Technician training pathways at undergraduate (pharmacist) and pre-registration / foundation training (Pharmacy Technicians and Pharmacist) levels.

The HEE investment strategy requires payments to be made across community pharmacies in England, to support the provision of training posts. The purpose of this is to secure the capacity and quality management of designated supervision.

  • Pre-Registration Trainee Pharmacy Technicians (PTPTs) 2 year

  • Pharmacist Foundation Training Programme (Yr.5) 1 year

    • Purpose and payment

    Purpose

    Purpose of this declaration form is to define:

    • The Expense Head for this Transaction: “Training Allowance: Pre-Registration Pharmacy Technicians.”
    • Services provided by the above-named Community Pharmacy (“Supplier”) with respect to the Pre-registration Trainee Pharmacy Technician Training Programme (“the Programme”) in England.
    • Health Education England (“HEE”) payments to the Supplier for Services pertaining to the Programme – in this case totalling £46,009 per trainee, per programme period.
    • The training programme period is 12 calendar months.

    Payment

    Upon completion of this declaration and receipt of an invoice, HEE shall pay the Supplier as described below:

    As a condition of Funding, HEE require a quarterly report from the Supplier. Reports will detail:

    • the number of pre-registration trainee pharmacy technicians employed,
    • any attrition up to and upon the date of report writing and
    • trainee progress update and any concerns
    • a summary of lessons learned from the perspective of trainee/s, supervisor/s and at an organisational level.

    HEE will provide reporting templates upon receipt of this signed declaration.

    Contact details for enquiries and support will be provided.
