NHS Pharmacy Contraception Service Opt in to the revised service

The NHS Pharmacy Contraception Service has been expanded to include the initiation of oral contraception. You should read the revised specification.

Contractors that are registered to provide the existing service and wish to continue doing so, must opt in to the new service by 29 February 2024.

Contractors that do not opt in to the service will not be complying with the service specification and will be automatically deregistered from the service with effect from 1 March 2024.

Claims for consultations up to and including the final date of service provision will be paid according to payment arrangements in the Drug Tariff.
