NHS Pharmacy Contraception Service (PCS) Opt-in to revised service

The NHS Pharmacy Contraception Service has been expanded to include the initiation of oral contraception. A revised specification is available here: link

Contractors that are registered for the existing service will be required to opt-in to the new service
by 29th February 2024.

Contractors that do not indicate their intention to opt-in to the service (or otherwise), will not be complying with the Service Specification and will be automatically de-registered from the service with immediate effect on 1st March 2024 . Claims for consultations up to and including the final date of service provision will be paid according to payment arrangements in the Drug Tariff.
