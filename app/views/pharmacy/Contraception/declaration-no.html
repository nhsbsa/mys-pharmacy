NHS Pharmacy Contraception Service Declaration to opt out of service

Read the following summary of terms and conditions and, if you agree, complete the details required and tick the agreement box.

If you choose not to accept (sign this declaration) your decision to opt out of the revised service will not be progressed.

If you decide to opt in to the new service instead, you must do so by 29 February 2024. You will be automatically deregistered from the service with effect from 1 March 2024 if you do not make us aware of your decision.

I declare that:


  • I wish to cease service provision and that I am giving 30 days notice of my intention to do so
  • I am aware that the date 30 days after my notice will constitute the final date of service provision
  • If my notice occurs fewer than 30 days before 1 March 2024, the notice period will reflect the number of days remaining before 1 March 2024 and the final date of service provision will be 29 February 2024.
  • I will continue to deliver the service, or, where I am unable to provide it, direct people to where they can access the service until the final date of service provision
  • I am aware that claims for consultations that were delivered up to and including the final date of service provision will be paid according to payment arrangements in the Drug Tariff
  • I am aware that claims for consultations that were delivered on any date beyond the final date of service provision will not be eligible for payment
  • I am aware the set-up fee I have been paid is a one-off fee and should I reregister my pharmacy in the future, my pharmacy will not be eligible to receive it again
