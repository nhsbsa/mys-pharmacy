Childhood seasonal influenza vaccinations Register for the community pharmacy advanced service Childhood seasonal influenza vaccinations from 1 October 2025 to 31 March 2026

The community pharmacy childhood seasonal influenza vaccination service is an advanced service that offers and administers a flu vaccine to children aged 2 and 3 years old through community pharmacies.

Read about the service and its requirements on the NHS England website

You must register your pharmacy to provide this advanced service. By registering you are confirming that you are:

  • ready to provide the service in line with the requirements of the service specification
  • registering for the ability to claim payment for the provision of this service.
