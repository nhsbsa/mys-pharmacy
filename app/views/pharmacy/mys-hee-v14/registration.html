Foundation Trainee Pharmacist Grant Payments Register for this service

Use this service to make claims for education and training contributions for Foundation Trainee Pharmacists (FTPs).

To use this service, you only need to register your pharmacy once and declare that you are claiming the training grant in accordance with Part XIII of the Drug Tariff.

Once you have registered for the service, you will need to register each FTP you wish to make a claim for. This will only need to be done once when they begin their training, and you will be able to make amendments to their record during their training.

For each FTP, you will need to tell us:

  • their name
  • their date of birth
  • their GPhC registration number
  • their training start date
  • their Designated Supervisor's name
  • their Designated Supervisor's GPhC registration number

You will receive your training grant payment monthly in arrears.
