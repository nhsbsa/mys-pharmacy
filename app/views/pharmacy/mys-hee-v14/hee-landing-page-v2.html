Monthly submission Submit a training allowance claim for trainee pharmacists

Use this service to submit or view a claim for the funded training of pharmacy technicians and pre-registration trainee pharmacy technicians (PTPT).

This service will let you submit monthly declarations to claim funding for each trainee. The trainee must be due to start or have started the pharmacy technicians or PTPT training.

You can use this service to:

  • start a new submission
  • view your current and previous monthly submissions
  • submit your declaration for funding
  • manage trainee details
  • add new trainees
