COVID-19 vaccination programme: 1 April 2024 to 31 August 2025 - Site selection process for suppliers About the location of your proposed site

Your proposed site should be where vaccination supply would be delivered and from where vaccines would be administered.

There will be flexibility to also vaccinate elsewhere with the prior consent of the Commissioner (for example a pop-up site). Vaccinations must only be offered where suitable facilities are available and patient dignity and confidentiality can be respected.
