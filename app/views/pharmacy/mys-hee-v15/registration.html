Foundation Trainee Pharmacist training grant and
Pre-registration Trainee Pharmacy Technician education and training contribution payments Register for this service

Use this service to make claims for education and training contributions for Foundation Trainee Pharmacists and Pre-registration Trainee Pharmacy Technicians.

Once registered for this service you will be able to make a claim for your trainees, for which you will receive 12 monthly payments.

You can read full details of the service and requirements on the NHSBSA website.

You will need to register each trainee you need to make payment claims for. This will only need to be done once when they start their training, and you will be able to make amendments to their record during their training.

For each trainee, you will need to tell us:

  • their name
  • their date of birth
  • what type of trainee they are - Foundation Trainee Pharmacist (FTP) or Pre-registration Trainee Pharmacy Technician (PTPT)
  • their training start date

For FTPs, we also require:

  • their GPhC registration number
  • their designated Supervisor's name
  • their designated Supervisor's GPhC registration number

To use this service, you only need to register your pharmacy once and declare that you will provide the service in line with the service specification.
