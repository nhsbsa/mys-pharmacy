NHS Pharmacy First Service Register for this service

The NHS Pharmacy First Service is a National Advanced Service to refer patients requiring low acuity advice, treatment and urgent repeat prescriptions to Community Pharmacies.

The service replaces the NHS Urgent Medicine Supply Advanced Service (NUMSAS) and Digital Minor Illness Referral Service (DMIRS).

Read details of the service and requirements on the NHS England website. More information can be found on the PSNC website.

You need to register your pharmacy to use this service.

By registering you are confirming that you are ready to provide the service in line with the requirements of the service specification.
