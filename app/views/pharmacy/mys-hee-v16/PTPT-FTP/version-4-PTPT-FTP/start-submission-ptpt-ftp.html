Foundation trainee pharmacist (FTP) training grant and
pre-registration trainee pharmacy technician (PTPT) education and training contribution payments Start your submission

Use this service to make claims for training grant contributions for Foundation Trainee Pharmacists (FTPs) and education and training contributions for Pre-registration Trainee Pharmacy Technicians (PTPTs).

You will need to confirm the details of each FTP or PTPT you are claiming for separately.

You should begin claiming for the training grant or education and training contributions at the start of the training period. That is the point at which an FTP or PTPT begins their training with you.

Payments are made monthly in arrears in accordance with Part XIII of the Drug Tariff for the FTP training grant, and in accordance with a signed NHS England contract for the PTPT education and training contributions.

For each FTP, you will need to tell us their:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • GPhC registration number
  • training start date
  • Designated Supervisor’s name
  • Designated Supervisor’s GPhC registration number

For each PTPT, you will need to tell us their:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • training start date
  • Designated Supervisor’s name
  • Designated Supervisor’s GPhC registration number

You must inform the NHSBSA if the training situation changes, for example if you cease to become a training site or if the trainee leaves your training programme.
