Sell a Prescription Prepayment Certificate (PPC)

Help your customers living in England save money with their NHS prescriptions, by selling them either a PPC or an HRT PPC.

Important: Important Information

  • Ensure you have sufficient authority to sell a certificate from either the owner or head office of your business.
  • The HRT PPC which only covers Certain HRT Medications. If your customer regularly needs non-HRT medication, a PPC may be more beneficial for them.
  • Inform your customer that if they are purchasing an HRT PPC, they will be unable to claim a refund.

Standard PPC

A PPC covers all the customer’s NHS prescriptions, an HRT PPC covers only the customer’s HRT prescriptions.

A PPC costs:

  • £30.25 for 3 months
  • £108.10 for 12 months.
