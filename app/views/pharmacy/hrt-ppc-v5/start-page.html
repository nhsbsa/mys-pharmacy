Before you continue Before you continue

  • Make sure you have the authority to sell a certificate from either the owner or head office of your business.
  • An HRT PPC only covers certain HRT Medications. If your customer regularly needs non-HRT medication, a standard PPC may be more beneficial for them.
  • Tell your customer that refunds are not available if they purchase an HRT PPC.

Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT) PPC

You can help your customer save money on NHS HRT prescriptions by selling them an HRT PPC.

Each prescription item usually costs £9.65, but prepaying for an unlimited number of HRT prescription medications costs £19.30 for 12 months, the usual cost of 2 medications.

After payment, we can send the certificate details to your customer by email or post. You can also give them the details to use yourself.

Your customer needs to show their certificate details when they collect their HRT prescription. They can use it straight away if they start it from today.

Prescription Prepayment Certificates (PPCs)

A PPC covers all the customer’s NHS prescriptions, whereas an HRT PPC only covers the customer’s HRT prescriptions.

A PPC costs:

  • £31.25 for 3 months
  • £111.60 for 12 months.
