Disclaimer

In accordance with Part VIC of the Drug Tariff, claims for chargeable activity should be submitted via MYS by the 5th of the following month (provisions for bank holidays are detailed in the Drug Tariff), with a grace period that differs for the two NHS prescribing services.

NHS Pharmacy First clinical pathway consultations submitted via MYS will only be accepted until the end of the following month.

NHS Pharmacy Contraception service consultations submitted via MYS will only be accepted up to 3 months after the date by which the claim should have been submitted.
