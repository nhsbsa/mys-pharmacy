COVID-19 vaccination programme: 1 April 2024 to 31 August 2025 - Site selection process for suppliers About your proposed service - IT user 1

New Designated Sites need to be set up on the IT systems that facilitate participation in the COVID-19 vaccination service as part of the necessary onboarding process. We need the contact information of two IT Users (with @nhs.net email addresses) to allow for vaccine supply and other accounts to be created.

If you are a site with existing IT Users and want to change these, please follow the process set out in the Mobilisation guide.

This must be an @nhs.net username of an individual (and not generic / shared email accounts). For ‘Other Providers’, if you do not have an @nhs.net email address, please leave this blank and you will be assigned one during the onboarding process.
